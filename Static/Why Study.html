Why Study in Malaysia



    In Malaysia, International Student life can be an amazing experience because of the many benefits that students get to use including low-cost living, diversity in culture and food, and even the weather. In the same time, it is the ease of travelling to nearby countries and experiencing South Asia and ease of language as English is widely spoken by the people of Malaysia. As an international student, the application process can often be long and exhausting, however, the process to enrol in Malaysiaâ€™s private universities as an international student is surprisingly straightforward and easy. Once you join as an international student in Malaysia, you will be sharing your student experience with 93,000 other international students from over almost 136 countries.

Affordable Tuition Fees

    One of many reasons for international students to continue their higher educations in Malaysia is that they will be surrounded by a diverse ethnicity group of people and have numerous academic courses at very affordable prices compared to other countries. Even in Malaysia, the degree courses tuition fees are pretty affordable that allow the students to study here and receive authorised certificates from the private universities' partner universities in different countries like in the United Kingdom and Australia. In terms of reliability, international students can trust private higher institutions in Malaysia because the Malaysian Qualification Agency (MQA) fully monitors the quality of all fields of the education system. This does assure the quality of private universities up to the mark and all the students will be to obtain necessary skills that are valuable in their related area of job. In Malaysia, even the students will be able to do a degree from an international university which originally based outside of Malaysia. For example, Monash University Malaysia students will pay Malaysian fees. As a comparison, for a Bachelor of Business Degree at Monash University in Australia, the students will need to pay approximately average tuition fees USD 45,000 whereas in Malaysia for the same degree, the international students need to pay USD 10,416 for Malaysian currency at Monash University Malaysia campus. This will help a lot the international students to save more money by continuing their higher education in Malaysia.

Affordable Cost of Living

    Over the past few years, Malaysia has emerged as one of the top study places for international students because of the low living costs in Malaysia. This advantage allows the students to gain sufficient time to enjoy, travel and experience a super side of independent living in Malaysia. The students can enjoy a good meal at $3 which helps save their costs very much here in Malaysia. Moreover, the transport is also pretty convenient for the students with public trains and buses running every day. Even the transport is very much affordable for the students to pay for their daily routine and public transport offering discounts specially for students. The rent is also very much affordable where the students even can rent a decent condominium at $ 130. In overall, the average cost of living in Malaysia is about USD 350 per month includes accommodation, transportation, foods, utilities, and other miscellaneous.

International Recognition Universities

    Malaysian both public and private universities are internationally recognised. For example, private universities like Talyor's University, UCSI University and Multimedia University (MMU) are placed rank in the QS World University Rankings. Also, University Malaya (UM) was placed at number 70 position in 2020 QS World University Rankings.
