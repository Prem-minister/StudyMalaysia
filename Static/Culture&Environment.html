Malaysia Culture and Environment

    Malaysia is a diverse country which consists of multiple ethnic races which shares different languages and religions. Each ethnicity has generally retained and continued their ancestors’ cultures and traditions. As a result, Malaysian society consists of a rich variety of lifestyles, beliefs and values. Among the different ethnic races, the Malay would consist the majority. Therefore, the general Malaysian culture tends to reflect the Malay’s culture and traditions. However, there are many other cultures and traditions that is being practiced in this multiracial society such as Chinese culture and Indian culture.
